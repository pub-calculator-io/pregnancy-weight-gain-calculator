Track your healthy pregnancy weight gain with our free calculator. Get a personalized weekly weight plan based on your pre-pregnancy BMI and IOM guidelines.

[https://www.calculator.io/pregnancy-weight-gain-calculator/](https://www.calculator.io/pregnancy-weight-gain-calculator/)

== Usage ==

Add the Pregnancy Weight Gain Calculator shortcode to your page, post or sidebar:

`[calcio_pregnancy_weight_gain_calculator]`

Add the following code to your template where you would like the Pregnancy Weight Gain Calculator to appear:

`<?php display_calcio_pregnancy_weight_gain_calculator(); ?>`

2. Activate the Pregnancy Weight Gain Calculator plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
